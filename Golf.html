PALM SPRINGS GOLF COURSE RESORT

It's Game on In Palm Springs

Miramonte Resort & Spa is surrounded by world-class golfing opportunities – next door and in the surrounding Palm Springs area.

The adjacent Indian Wells Golf Resort features the Clive Clark’s Celebrity Course, the famed Players Course and a Putting Course. This Palm Springs golf complex is home to two courses ranked in the Top 25 “Best Municipal Courses in the United States” and offers an excellent desert golf experience.

Guest Benefits

Book your tee time at Indian Wells Golf Resort up to 30 days in advance. Miramonte guests enjoy a discount when you book direct through the online reservations system: https://www.indianwellsgolfresort.com/book_tt/ Use promo code: mresort (all lower case). Miramonte guests also have access to convenient charging privileges at Indian Wells Golf Resort – simply show your room key and charges will be added to your existing method of payment at Miramonte. Go somewhere
The Chalets, Naldehra,Shimla

Nestled at an altitude of 3,044 meters, The Chalets, Naldehra, promise to provide a relaxing and rejuvenating experience to the guests. The 19 guest rooms in the hotel comprise of six long chalets, six apartments, three family chalets and two double bedrooms. Built with imported goods, these accommodation units are equipped with a range of modern amenities. If you are a golfing enthusiast, you can have a great time at The Naldehra Golf Course, which is located at a walking distance from this place.

Cambay Golf Resort Jamdoli, Jaipur

Located at a close distance from Jaipur International Airport, Cambay Golf Resort Jamdoli has become the favorite jaunt for golf enthusiasts. The range of interesting facilities provided makes it an ideal place for leisure travelers. It offers world class facilities to guests along with the thermostat A/C controlled rooms fitted with modern amenities. The main attraction of this resort is the nine-hole golf course.

Hyatt Regency Kolkata

A luxury business hotel in Kolkata, Hyatt Regency is the first five star hotels in the Salt Lake City acclaimed as one of Kolkata’s favorite. Featuring impressive architecture, it offers 255 rooms that are classified as Standard Rooms, Regency Club Rooms and Suites. Golfing at Hyatt is all about trying your hands at the gentleman’s game. There are 3 major golf clubs in Kolkata- The Calcutta Ladies Golf Club, The Royal Calcutta Golf Club and The Tollygunge Club. Each of these 3 golf resorts is 16-18 kilometers from Hyatt Regency.
