Afternoon Tea
We are no longer offering Afternoon Tea during July and August. We're now taking bookings for September onwards.

VEGAN and GLUTEN FREE afternoon tea are available on PRE-ORDER only.

Celebrate something special or indulge in a little treat with our decadent Afternoon Tea - Cardiff may be just a scone's throw away but with views to die for you'll be forgiven for thinking you're deep in the heart of the beautiful Welsh countryside.

Choose from our Traditional Afternoon Tea, Vegan Afternoon Tea or Gluten Free Afternoon Tea and enjoy a selection of sweet and savoury treats, traditional favourites like homemade scones and bara brith and, of course, a nice hot pot of your favourite tea from our speciality range.
