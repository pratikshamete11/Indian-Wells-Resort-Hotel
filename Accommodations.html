AMENITIES

Your Indian Wells Resort fee includes a generous deluxe continental breakfast buffet in Frank’s Place Restaurant daily, high-speed wireless internet access, complete privileges in our fitness center, complimentary shuttle service to neighboring Indian Wells golf courses, and complimentary entertainment fee nightly in Frank’s Place.

ALL ROOMS INCLUDE

  • Pillow Top Mattresses
  • Individual heat control
  • Individual air conditioning control
  • In-room safe
  • Rollaways upon request (in king rooms only)
  • High-speed Internet access
  • Flat screen LG TV
  • Direct T.V.
  • Voicemail
  • Hairdryer
  • Iron/ironing board
  • Coffee maker
  • In-room dining during regular dining hours

DELUXE ROOM

Spacious and newly renovated guest rooms have either one king bed or two doubles and feature contemporary desert design elements including plantation shutters leading to a private balcony or patio. Other amenities include flat screen tv’s and wireless internet access.
